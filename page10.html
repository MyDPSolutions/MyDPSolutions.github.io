Ordering Platform 

DPS works dynamically with your staff to place your orders and achieve great results.

What

DPS meets with manufactures and suppliers to negotiate product pricing.
We have secured exclusive deals with the suppliers and manufacturers, that way you know exactly what you are spending.  

Where

We work with independent and multi clinic practices in Canada. We have obtained reduced pricing due our relationships and volume of requests. While our supply partners enjoy the volume of sales and ease of access, our clinics enjoy the savings. This creates a win-win situation for both clinics and suppliers.

When

We operate on a two week ordering cycle. Clinicians enter their requests into the secure online ordering site any time during the cycle. If a need to order out of sequence, your agent will be there to conduct your request any time. You will receive a detailed purchase order for your records each ordering cycle.

Why

Purchasing can be a lengthy process. Staff must search through flyers and catalogues and make multiple phone calls just to obtain a good value each week. This can take HOURS away from your clinician’s valuable time. With DPS we do all the work for you, saving you time and money on all your purchases.

How

We handle every thing from Ordering to Returns.
Clinicians will be given access to our secure online ordering platform and can enter their order request any time. And that’s it! The rest is up to your agent at DPS! We seek the best possible price to get you the value you deserve.
