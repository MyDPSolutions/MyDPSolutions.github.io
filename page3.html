How we Got Started 

We established our business in 2011 during the up rise of dental group clinics. A group was being formulated with five clinics and discovered that exclusive vendor purchasing was limiting them in their volume discounts..it was then I formulated a strategy to represent this group and purchase on their behalf . We sought out multiple vendors to provide for that established a win-win scenario, for both clinic and vendor. This model has sustained this business and the group has tripled in size.
